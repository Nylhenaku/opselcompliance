About Us

 

ABOUT US
Opsel Compliance Consultancy Services Ltd is a UK based independent specialist financial services regulatory consultancy firm. We specialise in Financial Crime Compliance; Anti- Money Laundering (AML) and Counter Terrorism Financing (CTF), Sanctions, Anti-Bribery & Corruption (AB&C) and Fraud. We care about our clients' business, so we take time to understand their business and strive to offer solutions that are practical, appropriate and affordable. 
 
CORE VALUES
We are passionate about Financial Crime Prevention. Our objective goes beyond supporting our clients to comply with regulations, we also support clients to take a practical approach to fighting financial crime and reducing its harm on our society. Financial crime costs lives, wrecks communities, reduces trust and undermines national security. Its economic impact worldwide is substantial, that is why we are committed to helping clients prevent financial crime. 
 
OUR TEAM
We are a team of specialist trainers and regulatory compliance consultants with over 30 years of combined experience. Our trainers and consultants have worked for financial services institutions, regulatory consulting institutions and Law Enforcement Agencies. Our team is made up of individuals with global footprints across US, UK, EU, and Sub-Saharan Africa. We use our unique blend of experience and local knowledge and insight about the markets we work in to provide innovative and tailored solutions to help our clients design and implement effective AML/FinCrime programmes.
 


Nana Mante

Director

 

Nana is the Director and Lead Consultant for Opsel Compliance Consultancy Services Ltd. She has over 15 years’ experience in banking, payments and regulatory consulting, specialising in AML and financial crime.  

Nana is an ICA-certified compliance professional with a Diploma in Governance, Risk and Compliance and an MBA from the University of Westminster, London.

She is a former Business Compliance Officer of a UK based international bank and was responsible for the First line of Defense (FLOD) operational units, providing financial crime and regulatory compliance advice and guidance to the business.

Nana has deep understanding of both the operational issues and the key regulatory challenges faced across the payments and correspondent banking sector including areas of supervisory focus such as managing cross-border payments and international trade finance services.

Nana has extensive experience in working with regulated businesses in the UK, EU and in developing economies, especially Sub-Saharan Africa, offering independent Anti-Money laundering (AML) and other Financial Crime audits and training in all areas of AML and regulatory compliance.

With her background, extensive knowledge of UK & EU regulations and multi-jurisdictional capabilities gained from working with businesses operating in UK/EU and sub-Saharan African corridors, she is well-positioned to provide high quality advice and relevant support to businesses operating in these corridors on a range of regulatory compliance matters to help them to best managed their AML/financial crime risk exposures.

Grahame White

Grahame has been at the forefront of CTF Indicator Training and the concept of CTF Key Indicator delivery into Financial Institutions in the UK and Europe.

Former Head of European Operations for ACAMS (Association of Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialists) based out of Miami Florida.
